We are active in the continuous development and optimization of efficient methods for structural reliability analysis in collaboration with the Technical University of Munich. Below is a list of references on the reliability methods implemented in STRUREL and on application studies conducted with STRUREL.

We also offer training on probabilistic model building, reliability analysis and reliability-based design of engineering systems. For further information on specific training packages, do not hesitate to contact us.
